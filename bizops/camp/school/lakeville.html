The high school graduation rate of Lakeville Area School District #194 is impressive at over 93%, despite the district's 11,000 pupils. The Community Ed program is a favorite among residents and offers a wide range of classes and activities to ensure that learning never stops.

The University of Minnesota-Twin Cities, Carleton College, Macalester College, and St. Olaf College are all within a reasonable driving distance and provide a wide range of courses and degrees.
