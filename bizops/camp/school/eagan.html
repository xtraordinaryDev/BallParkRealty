One of Minnesota's best public school districts is the Rosemount-Apple Valley-Eagan Independent School District No.196, situated in ROSEMOUNT. With a student-to-teacher ratio of 17:1, it serves a total of 29,397 pupils from kindergarten through high school. In math, 64% of kids are at least proficient, and 66% of children are at least proficient in reading, according to state tests.
