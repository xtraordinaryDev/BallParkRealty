Rosemount

A Wonderful Place to Work and Live

Rosemount, Minnesota, is located about 15 miles south of the Twin Cities metropolitan area. People who live in Rosemount enjoy the benefits of both small town life and big city luxuries thanks to the city's land area of nearly 36 square miles. Unusual in its size, Rosemount is an excellent place to raise a family or work because of its mix of industry, agriculture, and agricultural research.

Western Rosemount demonstrates a dynamic, expanding residential community with an older, yet vital, commercial center at its core. Single-family homes, townhomes, condominiums, and low-cost housing are all viable options. It is expected that this pattern of growth will continue for many years to come.

Located in the heart of the city, Rosemount is a tightly knit neighborhood. It's easy to get around in Rosemount, which has plenty of undeveloped land, is close to Minneapolis and St. Paul and other major markets, has a good school system, and is home to many friendly people.
