Burnsville has a population of 61,311 and is located in the suburbs of Minneapolis. Burnsville, which is located in Dakota County, is often regarded as one of the premier residential areas in the state of Minnesota. Residents in Burnsville enjoy a lifestyle that is a hybrid of urban and suburban elements, and the vast majority of residents are homeowners. There are quite a few coffee shops and parks in the city of Burnsville. The population of Burnsville tend to hold moderate political views, and the city is home to a large number of families as well as young professionals. Burnsville's public schools are significantly better than the state average.

Overview of Burnsville
The senior high school in Burnsville, Minnesota, is ranked 147th in the state. Advanced Placement® courses and examinations are available to students. At Burnsville Senior High School, 31% of students take the AP® exams. Sixty-one percent of the pupils are minorities, and 36 percent of them are disadvantaged. A total of three high schools are located in the Burnsville Public School District, one of which being Burnsville Senior High School.
