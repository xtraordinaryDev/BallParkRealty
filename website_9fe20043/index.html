About Me

As a dedicated loan originator, my goal is very simple, to provide you with the best service by answering your questions and walking you through every step of the loan process. No matter if you’re a first time home buyer, seasoned buyer or just looking to refinance your home to a better rate, I’ll be here to find the best loan scenario for you and your family. As a former educator for 23 years, a current Real Estate Broker for over 18 years and a former Mortgage Broker, I’ve been able to apply my teaching skills to help many clients over the years and I look forward to helping you. Don’t hesitate to reach out using my direct contact information if you have any questions as I appreciate your business and I look forward to working with you!

My Mission

My mission is simple, to become the leading real estate professional in both MN and AZ. With my years of experience and proven track record, I believe that I can help thousands of families achieve their goals on both the Real Estate and Mortgage side of the transaction!.

My Vision

I believe in being the total package for my clients to help elimnate any confusion in the process. I envision making sure that you understand the loan process by using honesty and clarity as the true mark of excellence. Rates will change all of the time and by using up to date technology, I’ll be able to alert my clients when it may be a good time to lock in a rate or just wait. As your guide in the mortgage world, only the best scencerio’s will be presented allowing my clients to make the right move for their family!

My Why

I’m on a mission to make the transactions in both Real Estate and Loan Origination to be more transparent. I believe the more you know as a client, the more you’ll appreciate the end result when we meet our goal. Lastly, I believe that i can play a role in your family's success. These are the reasons that I strive to be a better professional in both Real Estate and Loan Origination!
